Winston is a very old dwarf who is a cleric. He is rather scrwany and fragile looking. Long white beard, tattos up and down his arms and legs. He always carries a decaying stick with carvings from top to bottom.

Working with house Tarkanan as an assassin. He started his life as a Life cleric. Did his best to keep people safe and healthy. Towards the end of the War of the Mark he devolped an abberent dragon mark. The house as part of their war tried to kill Winston. They ended up killing his whole church. After such events he devoted his life to 'assisting' people to the other side.
